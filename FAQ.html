FAQ about IIUM sisters' mahallah musolla


1) Why the building of Musolla Aminah is nowhere to be found?

Musolla Aminah is just a small room located in the mahallah's student lounge. The lounge is at the upstairs of the Mahallah Aminah's office.

2) Is there any mahallah that does not have a musolla?

There is no mahallah that do not have any musolla, but a few of the mahallah are sharing the same musolla; Asma' with Hafsah, Halimatus Sa'diah with Maryam, and Nusaibah with Sumayyah.

3) How can someone ask for permission to do any program at the musolla?

To book the musolla for any program, one can contact the mahallah office of respective musolla in order to ask for permission. Once the office allows, he or she can proceed with it.
